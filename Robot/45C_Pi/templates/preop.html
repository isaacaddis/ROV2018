Pre-Operation Checklist

  • Vehicle Inspection
    • Inspect for sufficient grease on rotatable claw-bearing
    • Inspect/secure all cable penetrators
    • Inspect all wiring epoxy on Servo Actuators
    • Inspect all wiring epoxy on Servo Actuators
    • Ensure all motor guards are tightly secure
    • Ensure the frame is securely attached
    • Ensure cap for Micro ROV connector and vent cap is secure
  • Tether and Tether Management Subsystem
    • Inspect the tether for visible damage
    • Ensure the tether cable is neatly coiled
    • Ensure proper connection from the ROV to the control box
  • Safety Inspection
    • Verify the location of first aid kits, fire extinguishers, and other safety equipment
    • Verify that the risk management plan developed earlier has been fully implemented
